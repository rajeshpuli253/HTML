Rahman is known for his extensive body of work for film and stage, for his stylistic range as a composer, and for his integration of assorted styles of music in his compositions.   • Sid SriRam Musical Event

Sidharth Sriram, popularly known as Sid Sriram, is a Carnatic musician, an Indian-American music producer, playback singer, and songwriter. He is an R&B songwriter and has been working in the Tamil and Telugu music industry and also in Kannada and Malayalam film productions.
